# AT Lore Quick Reference

## The World
- Mushroom War: nuclear/bio apocalypse ~1000 years ago. Created Ooo. Lich born from mutagenic bomb.
- Ooo: post-apocalyptic magical Earth. Rolling hills, kingdoms, dungeons, cosmic entities.

## Key Locations
Tree Fort, Candy Kingdom, Ice Kingdom, Fire Kingdom, Marceline's Cave, Lumpy Space,
Dead World, Nightosphere, Time Room (outside time), Islands (last humans),
Dungeon of Doom, Crystal Dimension, Mountain of Matthew

## Main Characters
- Finn: last human, raised by Jake's family, lost arm to Martin (deadbeat dad), heroic code
- Jake: stretchy dog, powers from alien father Warren Ampersand, married Lady Rainicorn
- Princess Bubblegum: grew Candy Kingdom from own biomass, genius scientist, loves Marceline
- Marceline: 1000yr old vampire queen, half-demon, survived Mushroom War as child alone, bassist
- Ice King: was Simon Petrikov (scholar), crown erased his mind, deeply tragic
- BMO: created by Moe (MO Co.), given a soul, rich inner fantasy life

## Cosmic Entities
- GOLB: chaos and non-existence. The crown's connection. Betty merged with GOLB in finale.
- Prismo: wish-granter in Time Room, literally grants wishes, is a dream of an old man
- Lich: pure evil/annihilation, born from nuke, goal = extinction of all life
- Death & Life: cosmic forces made manifest. Death rules Dead World.
- Catalyst Comets: periodic comets carrying cosmic purpose. Finn = comet reincarnation.

## Key Items
Demon Blood Sword, Enchiridion (hero guidebook, multiverse key), Magic Crown (corrupts/connects to GOLB),
Finn Sword (contains alternate Finn's soul→becomes Fern), Nightosphere Amulet,
Ancient Psychic Tandem War Elephant (legendary, very rare), Marceline's Axe-Bass

## Critical Relationships
Finn↔Jake (brothers), PB↔Marceline (confirmed romance), Simon→Marceline (surrogate father),
Simon↔Betty (tragic love across time), Jake↔Lady Rainicorn (married), BMO↔Finn&Jake (family)
